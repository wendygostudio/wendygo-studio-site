# SlimeForge 1.8 · Chrome Web Store descriptions

## English

SlimeForge is a Pomodoro focus timer where every session helps a tiny animal-slime grow — along with the world around it.

Choose 15, 25, 45 or 60 minutes, or set your own duration. Add a clear goal, assign it to a project or start one of your focus rituals. When the session begins, your companion appears on the page, sets up a tiny laptop, book or coffee, and works beside you until the bell rings. If you wander onto a distracting site, Focus Shield can gently warn you or firmly block it until the session is over.

Your first completed focus session begins the story. The egg that has been warming since installation hatches and reveals one of 16 possible animal-slime species. Every Pomodoro then helps your companion grow through its life stages and gradually reveals more of SlimeForge, so the interface starts simple instead of overwhelming you.

Complete daily missions, build a gentle streak, play minigames, grow food in the garden and unlock new looks in the Wardrobe. Adopt more creatures, keep them in your Stable and, with Pack Mode, let up to three companions walk and focus together across your pages.

Expeditions turn future focus sessions into journeys. Choose a route and an explorer, advance by focusing and return with materials, memories and relics. Discovering relics leaves a permanent mark on the diorama, adding special scenery as your collection grows.

When supported by your browser, optional on-device AI can help turn a vague goal into a practical next step, give your creature more varied conversations and privately interpret your weekly focus patterns. The model runs locally: your goals, sessions, browsing activity and conversations stay on your device.

SlimeForge is local-first, requires no account and includes no tracking.

The free version includes the complete timer, goals, basic Focus Shield, three projects, a focus ritual, daily missions, gentle streaks, care and minigames, a weekly summary and one free Forest expedition each week.

SlimeForge PRO adds ×1.5 Embers, advanced Shield schedules and ritual lists, up to 12 projects, intelligent weekly insights, the full three-creature Pack experience, a larger reserve, complete expeditions with species affinities and relics, exclusive cosmetics and three complete Forges per month. Try PRO for five active focus days — no card required. Eggs are never sold for real money.

Available in English, Spanish, German, French, Italian and Portuguese.
