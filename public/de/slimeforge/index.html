Focus. Hatch.
Watch it grow.

A pomodoro timer with a twist: every focus session you complete feeds a slime creature that hatches, grows and wanders across your web pages. Your focus, made visible.

Kostenlos, with a 5-day PRO trial included — no card, no account

Ein Begleiter, der durch deinen Fokus wächst

  • Full pomodoro timer: 15/25/45/60 minute sessions, breaks, weekly stats and streaks with bonus rewards
  • Completed focus sessions earn Embers 🔥 — the only currency; real money never buys randomness
  • Your first egg hatches with your first completed session; 16 animal-slime species across 4 rarities

  • Visit your friends' slimes with share codes — chemistry, shared memories, best-friend bonds
  • Pro: pack mode with up to 3 creatures interacting, The Forge creature editor, cosmetics, ×1.5 Embers and a Reserve of 25
  • 5-day free PRO trial included — no card, no account; when it ends nothing is deleted
