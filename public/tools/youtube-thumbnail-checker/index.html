    YouTube Thumbnail Requirements

    RequirementValue
    Recommended size1280 × 720 px
    Minimum size640 × 360 px
    Aspect ratio16:9
    Max file size2 MB
    Accepted formatsJPG, PNG, GIF, BMP

    YouTube accepts thumbnails as small as 640×360, but they'll look blurry on high-DPI screens. Always target 1280×720 for sharp results on retina displays and large monitors.

    The bottom-right corner of your thumbnail is covered by YouTube's timestamp label in search results, so keep faces, text, and important visuals away from that area.
