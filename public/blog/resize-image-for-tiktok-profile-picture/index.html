How to Resize Image for TikTok Profile Picture: Get the Perfect Square in Seconds

TikTok profile pictures need to be square—512×512 pixels for HD quality, or at least 400×400. If your image is rectangular, TikTok crops it automatically, which may cut off your face or logo. Resize correctly before uploading.

June 29, 2026

Your TikTok profile picture is the first thing viewers see in the feed and on your profile. If it is blurry, off-center, or cut off at the edges, you lose credibility instantly.

TikTok displays profile pictures as circles but requires them to be uploaded as square images. If you give it a rectangular photo, TikTok crops it to fit, often cutting off the important parts.

The fix is simple: resize to the correct dimensions before uploading.

TikTok Profile Picture Requirements

TikTok's exact specifications for profile pictures:

  • Recommended size: 512×512 pixels (HD quality on all devices)
  • Minimum size: 400×400 pixels (will appear blurry if smaller)
  • Shape: Square (aspect ratio 1:1)
  • Formats: JPG, PNG, or WebP
  • File size: Under 10 MB (usually not an issue)

Upload anything smaller than 400×400, and it stretches on mobile. Upload a non-square image, and TikTok crops the sides.

Why Resize Before Uploading

Let TikTok do the cropping, and you lose control. Center your face or brand logo in a 512×512 image first, and you guarantee it appears correctly no matter what device views your profile.

Creators and small brands especially benefit: a crisp, perfectly centered profile picture looks professional and encourages profile clicks in the For You feed.

Common Profile Picture Mistakes

❌ Uploading a rectangular image
TikTok crops it to square, cutting off your face or logo. Result: off-center, unprofessional appearance.
❌ Using a low-resolution image (200×200 or smaller)
Looks pixelated on HD mobile screens. Your profile picture appears blurry and loses credibility.
❌ Placing important text or logos near the edges
The circular display crops the edges more than you expect. Text or logos end up hidden.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size should a TikTok profile picture be?
The recommended size is 512×512 pixels for HD quality. The minimum is 400×400 pixels. Anything smaller will appear blurry on modern mobile devices. TikTok displays your profile picture as a circle, but you upload it as a square image.
Can I upload a rectangular image to TikTok?
Yes, but TikTok will automatically crop it to a square, which may cut off important parts of your image. To ensure your entire image appears correctly and centered, resize to a square (512×512) before uploading.
