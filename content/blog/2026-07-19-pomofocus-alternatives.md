Pomofocus is a clean browser-based Pomodoro timer — but it's a website, not an extension. It doesn't follow you between tabs, doesn't save streaks locally, and offers no reward loop for finishing sessions. These five alternatives fix that.

## What to Look for in a Pomofocus Alternative

- Chrome extension (toolbar access without switching tabs)
- Offline capability
- Session stats and streaks
- Customizable timer lengths
- Motivation mechanics beyond a plain countdown

## Best Pomofocus Alternatives in 2026

### 2. Forest — Best for Mobile Focus

Plant a virtual tree when you start a session; it dies if you leave the app. Available on iOS and Android. Trees accumulate over time; Forest also donates to real tree-planting programs.

### 3. Focus To-Do — Best for Task Management Integration

Combines a Pomodoro timer with a task list. Assign sessions to specific tasks and track how many sessions each task took. Available on Windows, Mac, iOS, Android, and as a Chrome extension.

Pomodoro app for Mac and iOS with customizable session lengths and Apple Reminders integration. No Windows or Android version.

### 5. Toggl Track — Best for Professional Time Tracking

Primarily a time tracker for freelancers and teams. Includes a Pomodoro mode. Useful if you already use Toggl and want session-based focus on top of billable-time records.

## FAQ

**Is there a Pomofocus Chrome extension?**
Pomofocus itself does not offer an official Chrome extension. It works in any browser tab but doesn't persist across tabs or appear in the toolbar.

**What is the best free Pomodoro timer Chrome extension in 2026?**
SlimeForge and Focus To-Do are the strongest free options. SlimeForge includes a full timer, virtual pet, and streaks at no cost. Focus To-Do offers free plans with task integration.

**What does Pomofocus lack compared to Chrome extensions?**
Pomofocus is tab-based — it doesn't run in the toolbar or follow you between pages. It has no offline mode, streak system, or gamification. A dedicated extension solves all three.

**Is SlimeForge completely free?**
The core timer, virtual pet, session stats, and streaks are free indefinitely. A 5-day Pro trial unlocks additional features; free features remain after the trial.

**Can I use a Pomodoro timer while browsing without switching tabs?**
Yes — Chrome extensions like SlimeForge open from the toolbar as a popup, so you never leave your current tab.
